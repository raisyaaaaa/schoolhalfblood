Pick your cabin

In Camp Half-Blood, cabins serve as living quarters for demigod campers, each dedicated to a specific Olympian god or goddess. Each cabin reflects the characteristics and mythology of its patron deity. The cabins provide a sense of identity and belonging, fostering camaraderie among campers who share similar backgrounds. Each cabin also has its own unique traditions and rituals, making them special spaces within the camp!
